About Me

I want to live in a world filled with books, dogs, and an endless supply of lentil chips.

As a student at the University of St Andrews, I study International Relations with a focus on human security, so that everyone is safe to live the way they want to.

When I'm not debating the ethics of international law, you can find me trying out a new recipe, hiking my way along the Fife Coastal Path, or asking someone if I can pet their dog.
